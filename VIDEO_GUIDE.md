## üé¨ Recording Tips for Smooth Video

### Before Recording:
1. ‚úÖ Close unnecessary programs
2. ‚úÖ Clear desktop
3. ‚úÖ Test microphone audio
4. ‚úÖ Use good lighting
5. ‚úÖ Increase text size (browser zoom 125%)
6. ‚úÖ Use 1080p or higher resolution
7. ‚úÖ Slow down your movements (click slowly, hover clearly)

### During Recording:
- **Slow Mouse Movement** - Viewers can follow better
- **Pause Between Actions** - 1-2 seconds to let it sink in
- **Narrate Clearly** - Speak slowly and pause after key points
- **Show Features** - Don't rush through functionality
- **Use Cursor Highlighting** - Helps viewers follow

### Recording Steps:
1. Open Music Player in browser
2. Record smooth demo (1.5-2 min)
3. Open Calculator in browser
4. Record smooth demo (1-1.5 min)
5. Total: 3-4 minutes ideal for LinkedIn
